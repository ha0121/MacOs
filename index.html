Hi, I'm Hao — a UX/UI designer dedicated to creating thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences that inspire people to live more intentional lives. With a background in both design and technology, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My goal is to craft products that are not only functional, but also deeply meaningful and blending aesthetics, empathy, and usability in every interaction. I believe that great design starts with understanding people. Whether I’m shaping a product from the ground up or refining an existing experience, I approach every challenge with curiosity, compassion, and a focus on clarity. My process combines research, storytelling, and iteration to create intuitive solutions that feel effortless and purposeful. Beyond the pixels, I’m passionate about mindful living, creative collaboration, and using design as a tool for positive impact. I’m always eager to connect with others who share the same vision and building experiences that not only work well, but also feel right.
